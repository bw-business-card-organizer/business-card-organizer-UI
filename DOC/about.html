ABOUT THE PROJECT

Stack is the modern solution to business cards. Ever missed an important connection because you didn't have any business cards on you? Ever lost a stack with a ton of important contacts?

With Stack, trading and keeping track of business cards has never been easier. Scan another user's QR code to swap cards with them, and never worry about losing contacts or expensive business cards ever again!
